Descriptions:

Customer Satisfaction Prediction is a data-driven approach that uses historical customer support data to forecast how satisfied a customer is likely to be after interacting with a service team. The goal is to identify factors that influence satisfaction, such as response time, resolution quality, rating , and communication channels, and predict the satisfaction rating before it is explicitly given by the customer.

The dataset has contains these informations:

Ticket ID → Unique identifier for each support ticket

Customer Name → Name of the customer submitting the ticket

Customer Email → Email address of the customer

Customer Age → Age of the customer

Customer Gender → Gender of the customer

Product Purchased → Product associated with the support ticket

Date of Purchase → When the product was purchased

Ticket Type → Category of the ticket 

Ticket Subject → Short description or subject line of the ticket

Ticket Description → Detailed description of the issue

Ticket Status → Current state 

Resolution → How the ticket was resolved

Ticket Priority → Level of urgency 

Ticket Channel → Mode of communication

First Response Time → Time taken to respond to the ticket initially

Time to Resolution → Total time taken to fully resolve the ticket

Customer Satisfaction Rating → Rating provided by the customer after resolution
